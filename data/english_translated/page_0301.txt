Did she mean to convey everything to Urmila before she returned home after finding out? Did Himu signal Prarthana to leave just as Jabeen had spoken after she finished talking? Did he end up in this state because he left the house with Prarthana? What did he say? Did Himavant tell her everything he had done after Prarthana spoke? Why such a sudden sense of peace in her voice? What a difference there is between her voice when she wailed, "Will Debu’s life be saved, Didi?" and now when she asks, "Is Himu better now?" What conversation happened between the two of them? What promise did Prarthana give? Did their broken friendship blossom again? What happened to Debu? "Tell Himu, Didi, that I have done everything he asked me to do; I have no other expectations now. Tell him I am just waiting for him to come to Hyderabad. He has become very emotional. When Prarthana finished, Urmila noticed Himavant fumbling for water. There was a different kind of light in his eyes now.

Urmila quickly went and brought a glass of water, took Himavant onto her lap, and fed him water bit by bit. There was a weariness on Himavant's face like that of a soldier who had lost a great battle.
