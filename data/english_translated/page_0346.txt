Some boys identified themselves with the novel's protagonist, Himavant, and similarly, many girls felt happy and shy, oscillating between the bit of Prarthana and Urmila in their own personalities. They would gently scold me. This novel, which was published as a weekly serial in the newspaper, was a topic of discussion in every household throughout the days it was published. How many boys have stopped me on the street and begged, "Please sir, don't betray Himavant. Make sure he gets Prarthana." How many girls have asked, "Are there really girls as heartless as Prarthana?" There were also those who said, "Publish this serial as a book soon; I need to get this to my girlfriend who left me." Is this a love book? Or the sigh of a wounded heart? - Ravi Belagere
