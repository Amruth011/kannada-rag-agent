How beautiful she looked! She had risen early in the morning, before even waking up properly, and had finished her bath in the well at the corner of the house. She knew that it was impossible to wash her long hair easily. Her hair was tangled like a snake that had been coiled up, and if it got caught in something, it would be a big nuisance. So, she had tied it up with a thread and wrapped a thin cloth around it, making it look like a bangle made of gold. If she had risen from the house of Shanthappa, her father-in-law, it would have been a common sight to see her standing in the courtyard, singing a song. But, if she was field, it would be a strange singing for the people passing by on the road. She was so engrossed in her singing that she didn't even notice when someone entered the room. "Oh, what a wonderful song she is singing! She must have been a great singer even when she was studying for her MBBS. It's not easy to learn music, even for someone with a good brain. But, becoming a doctor is not that easy either. If she had finished her studies and gone back to Shivamogga, it would have been okay. But, she is here, in Hassan, and is singing with such passion. Oh, how wonderful she is!" Shivamogga was saying to himself, completely absorbed in the beauty of Prarthana's singing. But, Prarthana was not singing for the sake of Shivamogga or anyone else. She was singing for herself, and her joy was evident in her voice. She was so lost in her singing that she didn't even notice when someone entered the room. with such abandon that it was as if she was singing for the first time. Her husband, Himavant, had written a letter to her, but she hadn't even noticed it. She had read it, but it had left her unmoved. She had seen the look of disappointment on his face, not affected her.
