Himavant was like a mole that had eaten its fill, and at that moment, she was as radiant as the Nandaka rain shower, making the day divine; she didn't even think of going to the house of dreams; she stayed in the eighth compartment itself, completely lost in her thoughts, measuring the threads of her life; Himavant was coming to life. In the courtyard of the garage, Prarthana was busy laying the lime plaster, not even noticing that she had stopped working; she was so engrossed in her thoughts that she didn't even realize become a human being, a being of lime plaster, dust and stone.
